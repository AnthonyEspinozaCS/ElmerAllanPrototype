Our Mission

The vision behind Elmer Allan's. A place like no others today. A place where those over 65 feel at home while getting an extra hand with groceries, appointments, and light medical services such as monitoring blood pressure and medications. High Quality Assisted living. At Elmer Allan's we will put the swing in living again.

Our Beginning

Dean was an only child whose parents had him late in life. Just like most people, Dean sees himself as having a natural interest in making the elder care sector the best that it can be. Everyone wants to make sure their loved ones are having the best care in their final years.

Our Story

As a child of older parents, Dean has naturally developed a kindred spirit with elderly people. His grandfather and uncle owned hotels and motels meaning Dean has been around the hospitality and accommodation industry since childhood. He knows what it looks like to operate in that industry with high quality management, housekeeping, and facilities. The assisted living industry has the same operational components and requirements for exceptional and reliable staffing and facilities.

Who We Serve

We strive to be more than just caretakers, but a second family for members of our community who are at the end of their life.

More Than Just Retirement

What if your last chapter of life could be spent in a light-filled facility with services including delicious and nutritious meals in your apartment or with others? Imagine sharing new and healthy activities with new friends while a care coordinator helped you remember your medical appointment and arranged for your transportation. What if you could truly continue to live your active life in a home that feels like home, with more services at hand and the ability to enjoy your retirement with the support to maintain your health? We aim to create a place that is a home and not just a senior living facility.

Our Values

We value excellent quality of care, modern facilities, and creating an environment where senior citizens can receive the long term care they require, but also live a healthy, active life style.

Meet Our Board of Directors
